# TORCS Autonomous Driving Learning Lab

## Welcome 👋

This folder contains the **TORCS Autonomous Driving Learning Lab**, a hands‑on, guided experience where you will explore how an autonomous AI driver works inside a racing simulator.

This is a **learning lab**, not a challenge or competition.  
The goal is to help you **understand, experiment, and reflect**, not to optimize for speed or rankings.

You will work step by step through the materials in this folder.

***

## What you will do in this lab

By completing this lab, you will:

*   Run an autonomous AI driver in TORCS
*   Observe how the car behaves on a race track
*   Modify Python code that controls steering, speed, and braking
*   Experiment with parameters and see real‑time effects
*   Reflect on what you learned and document your results

No prior experience with TORCS or autonomous vehicles is required.

***

## Learning mindset

Keep these principles in mind as you work through the lab:

*   There is no single “correct” solution
*   Going off track or failing is part of learning
*   Small changes can have big effects
*   Observation and reflection matter more than performance

Take your time, make one change at a time, and pay attention to what happens.
